Parking map created in QGIS for the Easton Business Association for Downtown Easton, Pennsylvania to make it easier for downtown visitors to find the best place to park.

Created with data from the [city's website](https://www.easton-pa.com/236/Parking-Department) and the [latest ordinance](https://ecode360.com/EA2741/laws/LF1946516.pdf) affecting parking rates in downtown as of March 2026.

## Parking Garages

- 4th St Garage upper floors $2.50/hr, first floor $2.50/hr, 2hrs limit
- 3rd St Garage. $2.50/hr

## Surface Lots

- 3rd St Surface Lot, $3/hr, 3hr limit (ParkSmarter only)
- Northampton St. Surface Lot, $3/hr, 10 hour limit
- St John's Church Lot, $2/hr, 5pm-8am only (ParkSmarter only)
- Social Security lot, $2/hr, 5pm-7am weekdays, 24/hrs on weekends (ParkSmarter only)
- Washington St. Lot, FREE. Weeknights after 5pm, 24/hrs on weekends

## On Street

Varies by location. More expensive closer to the roads leading up to Center Square. View map above for details.

## The Better Way to Get Downtown

Use the [LANTA bus](https://lantabus.com) to get to and from Downtown Easton - a more convenient and cheaper way of exploring what the city has to offer with convenient stops in Downtown Easton that are steps away from all the restaurants, bars, and attractions. Many lines stop at the Easton Intermodal Transportation center, which is located on the ground floor of the 3rd St parking garage. As of March 2026, 3 hour passes start at $2.50 and day passes at $5.
